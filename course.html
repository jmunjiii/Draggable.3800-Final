Course Description:

    This course allows students to build upon their knowledge of front-end development for websites and apps to produce complex, creative, and responsive sites incorporating interactive elements through the use of JavaScript and manipulation of the document object.

About this Project:

This project focuses on exploring and promoting a JavaScript library by developing a "How-To" microsite. The goal is to educate peers and the broader community on effectively installing and beginning to use the selected library. Through research and creative presentation, the microsite will serve as a resource for users, offering clear and concise guidance.
